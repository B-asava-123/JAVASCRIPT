Operators

Javascript operators are symbols that are used to perform operators on operands.

        1. Arthmatic Operators => Addition+, Substraction-, multiplication *, Division / , Modulus %, Increment ++, Decrement --

        2. Comparsion operators(Relational operators) =>Is equal to == , Identical (equal and of same data type) ===, not equal to !=, Not Identical !==, Greater than > , Greater than equal  to >=, Less than < , Less than equal to<=,

        3. Bitwise Operator => Bitwise AND &, Bitwise OR |, Bitwise XOR ^,  Bitwise NOT ~, Bitwise LEFT SHIFT <<, Bitwise RIGHT SHIFT >>, BITWISE RIGHT SHIFT WITH ZERO >>>

        4. Logical operators => Logical AND &&, Logical OR ||, Lgical Not ! 

        5. Assigment Operators => Assign =, Add and Assign +=, Substract and Assign -=, Multiply and Assign *=, Division and Assign /= , Modulus and Assign %=

        6. Special or Ternary Operators => condition? "true":"false";
